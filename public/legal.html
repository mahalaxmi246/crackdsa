Because our product is a digital service with immediate access upon purchase, returns are generally not applicable. However, we evaluate requests on a case-by-case basis under the following conditions:

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Here is exactly what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

What We Collect

When you use CrackDSA.in, we collect the following information:

  • Email address — used for authentication (via Google Sign-In or email/password)
  • Display name — shown on your dashboard
  • Progress data — which problems you've checked off, your streak count
  • Feedback messages — if you submit a feedback form voluntarily
  • Payment status — whether your account is free or paid (we do not store card details)

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Credit/debit card numbers or UPI IDs (handled entirely by Razorpay)
  • Phone numbers (unless you voluntarily provide one)
  • Location data or IP address logs
  • Browser history or tracking cookies
  • Any data from third-party apps or social media accounts

✅ We use Google Firebase for authentication and data storage. Your data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore, protected by Google's security infrastructure.

How We Use Your Data

  • To save and restore your problem-solving progress across devices

  • To respond to feedback you submit through the platform
  • To improve the platform based on aggregated, anonymised usage patterns

Third-Party Services

CrackDSA.in uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and database (Google Privacy Policy applies)
  • Razorpay — payment processing (Razorpay Privacy Policy applies)
  • Google Fonts — typography (no personal data shared)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third parties for marketing or analytics purposes.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, email us at support@crackdsa.in and we will permanently delete your data within 7 business days.

Children's Privacy

CrackDSA.in is intended for users aged 16 and above (primarily college students and professionals). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a minor has signed up, please contact us immediately.
